biography

Born in 1988, I grew up on Long Island. My love of music started in 3rd grade where I learned the viola. By 7th grade I had picked up the guitar, learning classic rock and blues. By 9th grade I learned the violin and piano, as well as music theory.

I attended CUNY Hunter where I obtained a Bachelors of Fine Arts in music. During that time I played jazz guitar in the Hunter Big Band, led by Ryan Keberle. I also played viola in Jekyll and Hyde off broadway which raised $50,000 for charity.

What I Teach

Strings, Guitar, Bass, Piano, Singing, Theory, Production.
